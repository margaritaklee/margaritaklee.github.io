hot momo
challenge
the objective of this assignment is to built a website for a restaurant that offers an online customizable menu exclusively for home delivery.
the first step is to decide what kind of restaurant and type of food would be a good addition to a specific area. this requieres a research of the existing restaurants with delivery serivce aswell as the food culture trends in the chosen area.
methodology
research: interviews, analysis of competition and trends, user flow testing
design: paper prototypes, lo-fi wireframes, clickable mockups
tools: paper and pencil, adobe cc, optimalworkshop, figma
process
i decided to focus on zurich, the city i live in and know farely well. the i started with an analysis of the food trends in the city and the exisiting restaurants that offer delivery service. the demand on fresh and healthy fast food is growing rapidly, aswell as the number of asian takeaway restaurants that aim white collar workers. i concluded that a momos restaurant would suit the purpose and the current demands.
the customizable manu is based on an analysis of the competition. after these fundamental decisions i continued to develope possible userâ€™s personas to identify the target audience and subsequently the character of the website. the next step was to develop a site map and flow map to define the navigation structure of the website. it was important to make the ordering sequence as fast and fluent as possible.
creation
i created a logotype that identifies with the origin of the momos - nepal. the nepalese writing is often characterized by horizontal lines that connect the elements in a word, which i used as inspiration. the color palette develops from a mood board created with pictures from nepalese landscapes, towns or characteristic food.
the menu is devided in three components each consisting of three items to chose from. the user can use the number selector the add items to the order, which must be confirmed before continuing to the check out process. the buttons are bright and clear, to make the whole order experience natural and effortless.
