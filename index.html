i’m an architect turned ux / ui designer focused on perfectly balancing functionality and design to create better experiences.

my background as an architect strengthens my skills as a designer to efficiently incorporate the client’s requierments into elevated design concepts while also focusing on functionality and usability. after 7 years of experience as an architect i took further training to extended my skills and become a ux / ui designer. i work with figma, invision and adobe xd to create mockups and prototypes. i also gathered basic skills on html and css.

i studied architecture at the bauhaus university in weimar and eth in zurich. after three years of working as an urban researcher i participated in several architecture design competitions both indepentently as well as while working at architecture offices in london and zurich. subsequently i carried on with planing and executing various building projects in zurich. on my spare time i took on some webdesign projects for friends and family.
